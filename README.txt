Multiplayer chess variant.
The rules are as in chess, apart from three exceptions:

   No turns! Each player can make a move at anytime.
   Each piece has a "cool-down": Once a piece has been moved, it cannot move again for x milliseconds.
   Castling and En Passant are not allowed.
   
   
I made a singleplayer only version that you can play here! https://lukesimmo42.github.io/kung-fu-chess-offline/
